"Although I remember Tomer, Lexi, and I would often spend time at Chabad meals discussing literature and a wide variety of topics, we really became more than just Chabad friends on November 13, 2011. It was the beginning of a relationship with my now husband Aaron, but also the true beginning of a lasting friendship with Tomer. Needless to say, he's been a part of so many important life events for this crazy group of friends: the Star Trek viewing party that started it all, Aaron and my wedding, when Spenser and Katie first met, and Spenser and Katie's wedding (and Ketubah witness!).
